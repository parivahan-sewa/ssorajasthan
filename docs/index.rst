=========================================
Rajasthan SSO ID Guide 2026
=========================================

Welcome to the comprehensive guide for the Rajasthan Single Sign-On portal. 

What is an SSO ID?
------------------
The `SSO ID <https://ssoidrajasthan.app>`_ is a unique digital identity provided by the Government of Rajasthan. It allows citizens, industries, and government employees to access over 100 different government departments and services using just one username and password.

Key Benefits
------------
* **Single Window Access:** No need to remember multiple passwords for different portals.
* **Service Availability:** Access services like Jan Aadhaar, Bhamashah, Recruitment Portal, and E-Mitra.
* **Secure Data:** Your personal information is verified via Aadhaar or Jan Aadhaar for high security.

How to Register
---------------
To create your own `SSO ID <https://ssoidrajasthan.app>`_, you can use any of the following:

1. **Aadhaar Card:** For instant verification via OTP.
2. **Jan Aadhaar:** Used for most citizen-centric services in Rajasthan.
3. **Google Account:** You can also link your existing Gmail to get started quickly.

.. note::
   Make sure your mobile number is updated in your Aadhaar profile to receive the verification OTP during registration.

Frequently Asked Questions
--------------------------
* **Can I change my SSO ID later?** No, the ID is unique and cannot be changed once created.
* **Is there a fee?** No, creating an ID on the portal is completely free of charge.
